Tuna Poke

Ingredients

  • 1/4 + 2 tablespoons cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 3 scallions thinly sliced
  • 1 pound sushi-grade ahi tuna diced into cubes
  • 1 cups sushi rice

Steps

  1. Whisk together soy sauce, vegetable oil, sesame oil, honey, ginger, and scallions in large bowl.
  2. Add the tuna into the bowl and toss.
  3. Refrigerate for 15 to 60 minutes.
  4. Scoop rice into small bowls and add the poke on top to serve.
