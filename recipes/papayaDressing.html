Papaya Seed Dressing

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/3 cup canola oil
  • 1/2 chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon papaya seeds

Steps

  1. Add the vinegar, oil, onion, honey, salt, and mustard into a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Slowly add the papaya seeds and keep blending until the seeds are ground up.
  3. The dressing is done and ready to serve on your salad!
