Mango Smootie

This is an original recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 2 cups worth of mango
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 3/4 cup milk

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into a cup.
  3. Add optional garnish if you feel fancy.
